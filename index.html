🔄 Recurring Sunnah Practices
📅
MON & THU FASTS
The Prophet ﷺ fasted Mondays and Thursdays: "Deeds are presented on Monday and Thursday, and I love my deeds to be presented while I am fasting." (Tirmidhi 747)
🌕
WHITE DAYS — الأيام البيض
Fast the 13th, 14th, 15th of every Hijri month. "Equivalent to fasting the whole month." (Nasa'i — Sahih)
🌅
MORNING & EVENING ADHKAR
Recite morning adhkar after Fajr and evening adhkar after Asr. Includes: Ayat al-Kursi, al-Ikhlas/Falaq/Nas ×3, Sayyid al-Istighfar.
📖
SURAH AL-KAHF — FRIDAY
Read Surah al-Kahf every Friday. A light will shine between the two Fridays. (Al-Hakim — Sahih)
